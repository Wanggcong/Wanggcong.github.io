Short Bio

Guangcong Wang is currently a PhD candidate of Sun Yat-sen University, advised by Prof. Jianhuang Lai.

His research interest is person re-identification, semi-supervised learning, and meta-learning. He has published several papers, such as ICCV, AAAI, and IEEE T-CSVT.

He currently serves as a reviewer of IEEE T-CSVT, IEEE Signal Processing Letters and Neurocomputing.
