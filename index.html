Short Bio

Guangcong Wang is currently a research fellow in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Nanyang Technological University, working with Chen Change (Cavan) Loy. Before that, he was a PhD student in Computer Science from Sun Yat-set University, advised by Prof. Jianhuang Lai.

His research interests are Generative Adversarial Networks, 3D, person re-identification, semi-supervised learning, interpretability of neural networks, and meta-learning. He has published several papers, such as IEEE T-NNLS, IEEE T-CSVT, ICCV, CVPR, KDD, and AAAI.

He has served as a reviewer of ICML, CVPR, NeurIPS, AAAI, ICPR, IEEE T-CSVT, Neurocomputing and IEEE Signal Processing Letters.
