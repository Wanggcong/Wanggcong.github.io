Short Bio

Guangcong Wang is currently a research fellow in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Nanyang Technological University, working with Prof. Chen Change (Cavan) Loy and Assistant Prof. Ziwei Liu. Before that, he was a PhD student in the School of Computer Science at Sun Yat-set University, advised by Prof. Jianhuang Lai and also co-advised by Prof. Xiaohua Xie.

His research interests include generative models, 3D, person re-identification, semi-supervised learning, interpretability of neural networks, and meta-learning. He has published some papers, such as IEEE T-NNLS, IEEE TIP, IEEE T-CSVT, ICCV, CVPR, KDD, and AAAI.

He has served as a reviewer of IJCV, ICML, NeurIPS, ICCV, CVPR, ICLR, AAAI, ICPR, IEEE T-CSVT, Neurocomputing and IEEE Signal Processing Letters.
