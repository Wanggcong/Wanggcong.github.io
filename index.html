Short Bio

Guangcong Wang is an Assistant Professor of Computing and Information Technology at Great Bay University. He is leading Vision, Graphics, and X Group (VGX). His research interests include 3D vision and AI4Science.

From April 2021 to April 2024, he worked as a postdoctral research fellow in MMLab@NTU at Nanyang Technological University, working with Prof. Ziwei Liu and Prof. Chen Change (Cavan) Loy. In December 2020, he received a PhD degree in the School of Computer Science at Sun Yat-set University, supervised by Prof. Jianhuang Lai and also co-supervised by Prof. Xiaohua Xie

He served as an Area Chair of CVPR 2026 and BMVC 2024, 2025.

He was awarded Excellent Doctoral Dissertation Award of China Education Society of Electronics.

He was awarded Outstanding Reviewer of ICCV 2023.

He is also a member of The Great AI Lab. Looking for self-motivated postdocs, PhDs, Masters, visiting students, and interns. Please drop me an email if interested. Link.
